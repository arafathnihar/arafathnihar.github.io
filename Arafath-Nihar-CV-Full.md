## Selected Projects

### Fitness Data Science

**Personal Project** | Apr – Jun 2024 | [GitHub](https://github.com/arafathnihar/fitness-data-science) | [Site](https://arafathnihar.github.io/fitness-data-science/)

- Built a public [R](https://www.r-project.org/)/[Quarto](https://quarto.org/) analytics project titled *Analyzing Fitness: Unveiling Trends and Insights from Personal Workout Data*, analyzing personal workout data from [Strong App](https://www.strong.app/) (sessions, sets, reps, weights) enriched with [Lyfta.app](https://lyfta.app/) exercise metadata (body part, primary/secondary muscles, equipment).
- Implemented `get_exercise_profile()` and `get_video_url()` scrapers using [rvest](https://rvest.tidyverse.org/) and [jsonlite](https://cran.r-project.org/web/packages/jsonlite/) to extract exercise metadata and video URLs from Lyfta pages; joined enriched metadata with workout logs by exercise name.
- Calculated training volume as weight × repetitions, aggregated by date, exercise, workout schedule (CSA, PPL), and body part; filled time-series gaps using [tsibble](https://tsibble.tidyverts.org/) for consistent progression plots.
- Developed visual analytics including [calendR](https://github.com/R-CoderDotCom/calendR) workout-frequency heatmaps (2023-12-01 to 2024-07-30), exercise-specific time-series progression plots, workout-schedule views, body-part volume bar charts, and exercise GIF displays.
- Published the analysis as a [Quarto](https://quarto.org/)/[GitHub Pages](https://pages.github.com/) website with reproducible source code; project configured as a DSCI452-style Quarto website with `docs` output directory.
